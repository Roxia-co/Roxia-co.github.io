Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis uses natural language processing and machine learning to interpret and classify emotions in subjective data.

Sentiment analysis is often used in business to detect sensitivity in social data, measure brand reputation, and understand customers.

Emotion analysis models focus not only on polarity (positive, negative, neutral) but also on emotions and emotions (angry, happy, sad, etc.) and even intentions (eg, interested or uninterested).

Topic Analysis

Topic analysis is a machine learning technique that organizes and understands large collections of text data, by assigning tags or categories according to each individual text’s topic or theme.

In other words, they enable you to find patterns and unlock semantic structures behind each of the individual texts.

Question Answering

Answer the questions to both closed (specific) and open questions (subjective). Answers to subjective questions is the main challenge for the likes of realistic Virtual Assistants..

Text Summarization

Summarizing a single or many articles according to a particular theme.

Text summarization refers to the technique of shortening long pieces of text. The intention is to create a coherent and fluent summary having only the main points outlined in the document.

Translation

Translate one language to another.

The task of automatically converting one natural language into another, preserving the meaning of the input text, and producing fluent text in the output language.

Speech-to-Text

Speech to text conversion is the process of converting spoken words into written texts.

This type of speech recognition software is extremely valuable to anyone who needs to generate a lot of written content without a lot of manual typing.

It is also useful for people with disabilities that make it difficult for them to use a keyboard.

CallExper

System that collectively controls and scores the conversations performed in call centers and obtains success determinations by sentiment analysis and language analysis.

System used for call centers to track their success and their agents success instantly.


Susam Sokağı - Museum Asistant

This artificial intelligence based museum assistant, where many artifacts found in today's museums are taught, presents the questions that the museum visitors are curious about the artifacts in a fast and with natural language.

It was created as a solution to problems such as reading long texts or struggling to obtain information in crowded museums.


Simurg - Caption Maker Automation

System works on producing subtitle support for stream-based video contents that are increasing and becoming a big part of our lives.

System that produces automated subtitles with language analysis in different languages with time coding for series, films and video contents or live broadcast contents.
